Ygeia: Where Health meets Harmony.

Rooted in the ancient Greek concept of "health", Ygeia represents a journey towards a balanced life.

Our mission is to ensure well-being that transcends geographical barriers. By providing essential health services in remote locations, at minimal charges, Ygeia aims to bridge gaps and bring vital health resources to underserved communities.

FAQs


  How are medicine orders packaged and sent?

Medicine orders are packaged by NGO volunteers; the medicines obtained from government-established generic drug stores at minimal charges.

  How are diet plans assigned?

The user first must take the preliminary questionnaire. The questionnaire records all preferences, diseases allergies and syptoms, based on which the application provides a suitable diet plan.

  What are the consultation timings?

The doctors are available during their daily consultation timings. However, they can always be contacted for emergencies.
